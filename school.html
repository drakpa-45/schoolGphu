Gelephu Higher Secondary School

Gelephu Higher Secondary (autonomous) School is located in the southern foothills of the country at an altitude of about 300m above sea level. It mainly serves urban community which falls under Gelephu Thromde in the local government jurisdiction which has a total area of 11.52 sq.km and a population of 13,339 (2014) with annual growth rate of about 5%.

The school is a comprehensive day school covering about 11 acres of total land area and enrolling approximately 870 students in classes IX through XII. The school has one administrative building, four academic buildings with six classrooms each, four laboratory rooms, one library room and a store. The school also has a multi-purpose hall with indoor games facility, a soccer field with cricket, volleyball court, basketball court and a futsal field. The youth centre is immediately adjacent to school and is widely used by our students. Currently, the school offers full courses in Science, Commerce and Arts streams. Besides, the school also boasts of having large green grassy areas and perennial gardens making one of the best school campuses in the country.

The Foundation Stone for Gelephu HSS was laid on 2nd June2001 with funding from the RGOB. On completion of the construction in 2003, the school was taken over by the Royal Bhutan Army for security reasons. In the following year (2004), the school was handed over to Ms. Sangay Zam, who became the first principal of Gelephu Middle Secondary School with classes from VII- X.

The school was upgraded to a Higher Secondary School in 2005 offering only two streams: science and commerce. In 2006, Mr. Pema Chedup took over the school as an Officiating Principal during which classes VII and VIII were phased out. In 2007, Ms. Yanki Dem took charge of the school till 2011.The school is presently under the leadership of Mr. Shambay Dawa.

The School Vision, Mission and Core Values

Vision:

A premier educational Institution for holistic learning.

Mission:

To nurture and develop our pupil’s potential and character through varied enriching programmes to face their future endeavors.

Core Values

Patriotism: Every individual will conduct themselves with commitment, loyalty, dedication and sincerity

Perseverance: Every individual will not let failure defeat them , instead they will endeavor to gain success

Preservation:Every individual will work and contribute towards preservation of culture, tradition and environment

Preparedness: We will prepare every individual to become academically competent, physically fit and morally sound through quality education.

Personality: Character building through continuous guidance.
